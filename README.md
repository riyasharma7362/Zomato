    <h3>Account Setup</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Sign up for a new account or log in with your existing credentials.</li>
        <li>Complete your profile to receive personalized recommendations.</li>

    <h2 id="usage">Usage</h2>
    <h3>Discovering Restaurants</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Use the search bar to find restaurants by name, cuisine, or location.</li>
        <li>Apply filters to narrow down your search.</li>
    </ul>
    <h3>Reading Reviews</h3>
    <p>Select a restaurant to view detailed information, including user reviews and ratings.</p>
    <h3>Making Reservations</h3>
    <ol>
        <li>Select a restaurant that offers reservations.</li>
        <li>Choose your preferred date and time, and confirm the reservation.</li>
    </ol>
    <h3>Ordering Food</h3>
    <ol>
        <li>Select a restaurant that offers delivery or takeout.</li>
        <li>Browse the menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout.</li>
        <li>Choose a payment method and place your order.</li>
    </ol>
